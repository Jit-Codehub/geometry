What is Reflex or Conjugate Angles?

In algebra, if you add any two angles, the sum of the two angles should be 360° or π then it is called as reflex or conjugate angles. Reflex angles always have the positive measures.

For example, 190° + 170° = 360°.

Reflex or Conjugate Angle Formula

Look into the formula to calculate the conjugate angles easily.

In Degrees,

Conjugate Angles = 360° - x

In Radians,

Conjugate Angles = 2π - x
